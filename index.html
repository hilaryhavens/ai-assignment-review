About the IDEA V7 framework

This tool builds a prompt that asks an AI to analyze your assignment across three layers — Cognitive Demands, Student Cognitive Engagement, and AI Robustness — then propose Low / Medium / High-effort redesigns and a before/after learning-robustness score. Nothing you type is uploaded: your library is saved only in this browser. Scanned (image-only) PDFs have no extractable text — paste the text instead.
